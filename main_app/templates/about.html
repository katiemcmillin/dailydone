DailyDone

DailyDone is a streamlined project management tool crafted for efficiency and superb user-experience. Enabling users to easily create, track, update, and complete tasks and projects, it injects simplicity into every step of project management. The platform fosters productivity and team collaboration by providing features for task assignments, timeline creation. With the profile feature, users gain a personalized experience. The dashboard provides an overview of ongoing tasks, upcoming deadlines, and completed achievements, giving users a comprehensive picture of their productivity landscape.

Whether managing personal tasks or coordinating team-based projects, DailyDone offers a comprehensive solution to visualize and monitor progress seamlessly. With its user-friendly interface and a wealth of features, it's an optimal choice for anyone seeking a superior approach for project management and task tracking. With DailyDone, we aim to turn every day into a day of accomplishment.

Key Features:

User Authentication

Register, Login, Logout, Create and Edit User Profile

Project Management

Create, View, Edit, and Delete projects, Set Project's Characteristics, Monitor Project's Status, Assign Admin and Contributors to projects, Mark them as as completed

Task Management

Add, View, Update, and Delete tasks, Set Task's Characteristics within projects, , Monitor Task's Status, Assign Admin and Contributors to tasks, Mark them as as completed

Collaboration

Assign tasks to specific unique or multiple contributor(s) and invite them to join projects and tasks, display list of Contributors and Admin per project and task

User Profile

With our user profiles, each individual can tailor their experience to fit their needs, enhancing productivity and providing a personalized touch to project management
